    About

    Aqua World welcomes you to the world of Live Aquatic Plants, Fresh Water Fishes, Aquarium Accessories. We offer the high quality Aquatic Plants and Fishes that grows with in the Venice of the east, Alappuzha. We have more than 600+ farm products. Our Aquatic Plants are harvested in the same day we ship. Everything is Fresh from our Farm !.

    The functioning of this firm started in 2015 December with 1 aquarium and a mini farm. Now this firm has two farms and one farm outlet. In the beginning the supply was restricted to Kerala only now company has spread its supply to all over India. The registered office of our company situated at Kadavanthara, Kochi, Kerala, India.
